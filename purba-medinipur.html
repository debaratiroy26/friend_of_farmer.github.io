Purba Medinipur

Purba Medinipur district is an administrative unit in the Indian state of West Bengal. It is the southernmost district of Medinipur division – one of the five administrative divisions of West Bengal. The headquarters in Tamluk.

Soil & Rain Report

Main source of income in the district is from the agriculture sector and per capita income is Rs. 87,082.Total cropped area is 5,30,613 in hectares and the forest area is 820.05 in sq km (2019).The Major crops grown in this district are paddy ( Aus , Aman & Boro) , Pulses , Oilseeds , Potato , Vegetables , Betelvine , Flowers etc. . The marketing mainly depend on local market and nearby Kolkata market. Use of Fertilizer, Improved seeds & pesticides are increasing day by day.
