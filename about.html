World Water Day

The United Nations declared March 22 to be celebrated as World Water Day.

International Seeds Day

It was initiated by farmers worldwide as a gesture to promote biodiversity, patent-free and GMO-free seeds, rights of farmers, and to challenge Order 81, which was a “declaration of war against farmers” passed on April 26, 2004.

World Soil Day

Celebrated on December 5 to highlight how essential healthy soil is to sustainably maintain soil resources.

National Farmers’ Day/Kisan Diwas

The government initiated the celebration of Kisan Diwas on December 23 ,2001 to recognize the contribution of Choudhary Charan Singh, who is the most reputable farmers’ leaders in India.
