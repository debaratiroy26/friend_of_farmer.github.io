Malda

Malda district, also spelt Maldah or Maldaha is a district in West Bengal, India. It lies 347 km (215 miles) north of Kolkata, the capital of West Bengal. Mango, jute and silk are the most notable products of this district. The special variety of mango, Fazli, produced in this region is popularly known by the name of the district and is exported across the world and is internationally acclaimed.

Soil & Rain Report

Rice, jute, legumes, and oilseed are the chief crops in the surrounding area.
Malda is the largest producer of excellent quality jute and mango in India.
